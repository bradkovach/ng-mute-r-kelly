Frequently Asked Questions

What is this?

It's a web app that will quickly go through your Spotify playlists, detect and remove R. Kelly music. It also removes any music that R. Kelly wrote or any tracks he was featured on.

Why?

R. Kelly has a long history of sexual imprisonment and abuse. Every time we play his music, we give him power. The idea came after I watched Surviving R. Kelly on Lifetime.

How do I use it?

You can automatically remove R. Kelly from all playlists, or one playlist at a time. Once you're logged in, go to Playlists and click Auto Remove, or go to an individual playlist and Auto Remove.

Is this safe/secure?

Yes. Every effort has been made so that your credentials never leave your browser window. Simply refresh the page or close your tab and access to your Spotify account will disappear automatically.

Your Spotify credentials (username/password/application access tokens/etc) are not stored and they never leave your browser. If you want, you can remove the app from your Spotify account from your Spotify Apps Page.

Can I review the source code?

Yes! The project is open source, and you are invited to contribute to the utility. You can Contribute on GitHub!
